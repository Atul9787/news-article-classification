Before joining ESPN Los Angeles, Brian and Andy hosted Lakers Blog for the Los Angeles Times from 2005 to 2009. They host the ESPN Kamenetzky Brothers Lakers PodKast and frequently appear on ESPN Radio 710. As contributors for ESPN The Magazine and ESPN.com, they have written about virtually every sport known to mankind.
